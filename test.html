Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is 0xPoland?

    0xPoland is a series of meetups, workshops and hackathons for Ethereum developers, organized by Ethworks

  • Who is 0xPoland for?

    Our meetups are suited for beginner and advanced developers who want to level up their skills in Ethereum coding.

  • Where do the events take place?

    The events will be hosted online. We hope to be able to host a real world event soon!

  • What's the purpose of 0xPoland?

    Our goal is to build a thriving community of Ethereum developers in Poland, eager to learn new skills and share their knowledge.

  • What's after 0xPoland Season 1?

    Season 1 will end with a hackathon, and we are currently planning a lot cool stuff for season 2!
